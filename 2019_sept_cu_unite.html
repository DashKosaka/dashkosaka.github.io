CU Unite: Connecting the Champaign-Urbana Community

In September 2019, CU Unite was developed during the Pyghack 2019 hackathon to create a platform that helps visualize and analyze student organizations and their impact on campus. By offering an interactive dashboard, CU Unite enables both students and faculty to gain insights into how well organizations are contributing to the community and fostering involvement.

What is CU Unite?

CU Unite is a community-focused platform designed to bring together students, faculty, and others in the Champaign-Urbana (CU) area. It empowers users to start initiatives, gain visibility, and receive the support they need to make a difference. The interactive dashboard allows organization monitors to track the success and involvement of various student organizations, ensuring that they are actively contributing to the CU community.

Preview of the dashboard for a single organization

Key Features of CU Unite:

  • Interactive Dashboard: Provides a clear view of student organizations, their activities, and overall contribution to campus life.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Helps organization monitors ensure that student organizations are achieving their goals and positively impacting the community.
  • Community Building: Unites students, faculty, and local volunteers through a shared platform aimed at increasing engagement and collaboration.

Displaying individual and their respective organizations

Future Plans

We plan to expand the CU Unite platform, offering it to local volunteer organizations as a way to gain momentum and visibility. By next fall, we aim to launch CU Unite across the Champaign-Urbana community. If the test case is successful, we envision scaling the platform to more urban areas where it could have an even greater impact.
