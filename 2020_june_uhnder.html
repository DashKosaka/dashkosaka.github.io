System Engineer - Radar Feature Development, Uhnder

Summary

In June 2020, during the peak of Covid, I moved to Austin, Texas, to join the automotive radar startup Uhnder along with my team from our previous startup, PreSense. Uhnder was focused on disrupting the radar industry with a digitally modulated radar-on-chip technology offering unprecedented configuration, resolution, and interference mitigation capabilities. I was a founding member of the perception team, where I focused on researching and rapidly prototyping new perception algorithms that enhanced radar output usability and reliability.

Occupancy Grid Map (OGM) using an Uhnder radar

Key Contributions

  • Perception Algorithms: Led the research and rapid prototyping of new perception algorithms in Python, C++, and CUDA, designed to solidify the performance of Uhnder's radar.
  • Angular Resolution Enhancement: Developed an algorithm using LAP point matching to improve the angular resolution of point cloud data by over 2x.
  • Radar Pose Correction: Designed and integrated a method for continuously estimating and correcting radar pose to within a tenth of a degree.
  • Multi-Object Tracking: Owned the architecture and testing of a scalable multi-object tracking algorithm, along with the creation of its C++ API.
  • Bayesian Mapping Algorithm: Modeled and architected an asynchronous multi-sensor Bayesian mapping algorithm that achieved sub-meter accuracy for spatial occupancy and continuous velocity tracking, with a 90% runtime improvement using CUDA.
  • ROS Integration: Successfully integrated five perception algorithms into Uhnder’s ROS stack as separate ROS Melodic nodes, utilizing Python and C++.

Freespace estimation of surrounding environment using perception algorithms

Challenges

  • Overcoming technical hurdles in radar feature development while rapidly prototyping and testing new algorithms for enhanced radar perception.
  • Collaborating across teams to integrate cutting-edge perception features into a novel digital radar chip.
  • Balancing customer presentations, feature demonstrations, and R&D to drive investor support while ensuring the scalability of the technology.
